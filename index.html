Production Deployment

  1. Copy `.env.example` to `.env` and fill strong secrets.
  2. Edit `miningcore/config.json` payout and RPC credentials.
  3. Run `./scripts/setup-production.sh` on your Contabo server.
  4. Point hashrate to port 3333 (pool) or 3334 (solo).
  5. Add TLS/reverse proxy for `sam256.com/mining/` after validation.
